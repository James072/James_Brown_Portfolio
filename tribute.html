This particular project is aptly named Tribute website. This was more than just a creation to display my frontend development skills. This was a personal endeavor to memorialize and pay tribute to my own father, Reverend James J Brown Jr., while I was going through the process of grieving his death. He passed away on June 1, 2020. It started out as a way to deal with my own loss, but it turned into a method to help the other people who loved him get through their grieving process as well. It consists of three pages - an introduction page, a brief biography page, and the reflections page that consists of about 60 images of family and friends who held him dear and near to their hearts. As for the development of the site, I used HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5 and implemented JavaScript to process viewing of images on the reflections page.
